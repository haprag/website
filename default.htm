About Us

The Prag Lab at The University of Manchester, is dedicated to pioneering research in mitochondrial biology and pharmacology. We focus on understanding how mitochondria work in health and disease, including neurodegenerative disorders, cancer and cardiometabolic diseases, and harness this knowledge to develop novel therapeutics.
